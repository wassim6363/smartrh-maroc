# Payroll Disclaimer

CNSS, AMO, IR brackets, professional expenses, and payroll settings are database-configurable.

No Moroccan legal payroll rate should be treated as final because it appears in demo seed data.

Before production, all payroll rules, rates, ceilings, calculations, and payslip formats must be verified by a Moroccan accountant or qualified payroll expert.

Formula documentation is available in `docs/PAYROLL_FORMULAS.md`.
