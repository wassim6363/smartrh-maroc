# SmartRH Maroc Overview

SmartRH Maroc is a Moroccan B2B SaaS for HR and payroll operations. It targets SMEs, private schools, restaurants, call centers, fiduciaries, and accounting firms.

Core modules: companies, employees, departments, positions, absences, leave requests, payroll periods, payslips, HR documents, employee portal, subscriptions, support, and audit logs.

Pricing demo plans: Starter 149 DH, Business 349 DH, Pro 599 DH, Cabinet 999 DH.

MVP scope: demo-ready workflows, configurable payroll rules, private PDFs, CSV import/export, and company-scoped administration.

Payroll hardening now includes legal settings, IR brackets with effective dates, seniority bonus rates, employee payroll items, annual cumuls, and fiscal-only professional expenses.

See `docs/PAYROLL_FORMULAS.md` for the formula details and `docs/PRODUCTION_CHECKLIST.md` before deployment.
